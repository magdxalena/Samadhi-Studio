Samadhi Studio

Hello and a warm welcome! I'm Magdalena, a certified massage therapist and yoga teacher dedicated to helping you connect with yourself, release tension, improve strength and flexibility, and cultivate inner peace.

My passion for bodywork and yoga began about eight years ago. At that time, I was working as a graphic designer and turned to meditation, yoga, and massage to calm my mind and improve my posture. Soon, I discovered that there was so much more to it. After experiencing the transformative power of integrating body and mind practices in my own life, I decided to deepen my knowledge, complete various training courses, and share these benefits with others.

I believe in a holistic approach to health, where mind, body, and spirit are interconnected. In my practice, I blend different massage techniques with the principles of yoga and other healing practices to create the best experience for you. Whether you're seeking relief from physical pain or a break from daily stress, I'm here to help you.
